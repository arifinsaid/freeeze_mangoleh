Our Background

Our company based in Bandar Hilir, Melaka offers sweets with lined fruit flavored yogurt and served with a fruit as a topping. This uniqueness is definitely the number 1 choice for the locals. The start of this company is the result of trading yogurt in the night market on a small scale. After being inspired, its founder, Muhd Aliff wanted to open a special shop for his business in early 2020. The state of Melaka was chosen because of its many tourists and many marketing targets.

Lots of Options for All of You

There are many yogurt options you can try and will surely satisfy you all. Flavors include kiwi, mango, blackberry, strawberry, blueberry, grape, pineapple and more!
